About

Dhanush, born Venkatesh Prabhu Kasthuri Raja, is an acclaimed Indian actor, producer, director, lyricist, and playback singer. Primarily active in Tamil cinema, he has expanded his reach to Hindi films like *Raanjhanaa* and *Atrangi Re*. With numerous awards to his name, Dhanush has featured in over 46 films.
