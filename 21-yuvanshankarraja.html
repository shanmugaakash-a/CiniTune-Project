About

Yuvan Shankar Raja is a singer-songwriter, film music composer, lyricist from Chennai, Tamil Nadu. He has predominantly works in Tamil film Industry. He began his career at age of 16, in the movie - Aravindhan. He rose to fame with his famous track ‘Thulluvadho Ilamai’. Yuvan Shankar Raja is often credited to having introduced Hip Hop to the Tamil film and music industry and having started the "era of remixes" in Tamil Nadu. Yuvan Shankar Raja is the youngest son of leading Indian film composer Ilaiyaraaja.
