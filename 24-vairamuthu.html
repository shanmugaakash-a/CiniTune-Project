About

Vairamuthu Ramasamy is a renowned Indian poet, lyricist, and novelist who is active in the Tamil film industry. He is highly regarded in the Tamil literary community and holds a master's degree from Pachaiyappa's College in Chennai. Initially, he worked as a published poet and a translator.
