About

G. V. Prakash Kumar is a music composer, actor and singer. He has has extensively worked in the Tamil film Industry. His first film was ’Veyyil’, which released in 2006. He won critical acclaim for his music in Selvaraghavan's drama film ‘Mayakkam Enna’, which became his third collaboration that featured Dhanush in the lead role.
