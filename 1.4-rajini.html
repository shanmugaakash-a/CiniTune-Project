Kaala

Actor: Rajinikanth

Director: Pa. Ranjith

Description: *Kaala* is a 2018 Indian Tamil-language action drama where Rajinikanth plays Karikaalan, a don who fights for the rights of oppressed people in Dharavi. The movie delves into themes of power, class struggle, and justice, making it a captivating watch. Rajinikanth shines as the fearless leader who stands against corruption and injustice, while also protecting his community from a dangerous mafia. With a social message at its core, *Kaala* is not only entertaining but also thought-provoking.

Rating: 7.9/10
