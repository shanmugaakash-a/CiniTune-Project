About

Rajinikanth, born Shivaji Rao Gaikwad, is a highly acclaimed Indian film actor, producer, and screenwriter, primarily working in the Tamil film industry. He has appeared in over 160 films spanning a career of more than five decades. Rajinikanth is widely regarded as one of the most popular and influential actors in Indian cinema, known for his unique acting style, charisma, and mass appeal. He has won numerous awards for his performances and contributions to the film industry, including several Filmfare Awards and the Padma Bhushan and Padma Vibhushan, two of India's highest civilian honors.
