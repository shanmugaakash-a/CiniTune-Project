About

Ajith Kumar is an Indian actor who works predominantly in Tamil cinema. He has starred in over 60 films and received several prestigious awards, including four Vijay Awards, three Cinema Express Awards, three Filmfare Awards South, and three Tamil Nadu State Film Awards.
