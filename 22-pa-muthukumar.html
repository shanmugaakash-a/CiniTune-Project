About

Nagarajan Muthukumar was a celebrated poet, lyricist, and author from Tamil Nadu, India. He was widely acclaimed for his contributions to Tamil cinema through his film songs, and he won the Filmfare Award for Best Lyricist in Tamil more times than any other artist. In addition to his numerous Filmfare Awards, he was also a two-time recipient of the National Film Award for Best Lyrics, which he won for his work on the films Thanga Meenkal and Saivam.
