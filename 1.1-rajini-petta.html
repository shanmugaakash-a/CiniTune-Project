Petta

Actor: Rajinikanth

Director: Karthik Subbaraj

Description: petta is a 2019 Indian Tamil-language action drama film directed by Karthik Subbaraj. The movie stars superstar Rajinikanth in the lead role as Kaali, a mysterious and charismatic hostel warden with a hidden past. As Kaali steps into his new role, his actions soon attract the attention of dangerous people from his previous life. The film showcases Rajinikanth's signature style, blending action, drama, and humor with a gripping storyline. With an ensemble cast including Nawazuddin Siddiqui, Vijay Sethupathi, and Simran, *Petta* offers an exhilarating experience for fans of high-octane entertainment.

Rating: 8.2/10
