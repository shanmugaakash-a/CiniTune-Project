Vettaiyan

Actor: Rajinikanth

Director: P. Vasu

Description: *Vettaiyan*, a character from the 2005 Tamil film *Chandramukhi*, is portrayed by Rajinikanth in one of his most iconic roles. Vettaiyan is a ruthless king who seeks revenge on his love interest and will stop at nothing to win her over, even after his demise. The movie blends elements of horror, thriller, and drama. Rajinikanth's portrayal of the dual role, particularly Vettaiyan, received widespread praise for its intensity, humor, and commanding screen presence. The film became a milestone in Tamil cinema, leaving an indelible mark on audiences.
