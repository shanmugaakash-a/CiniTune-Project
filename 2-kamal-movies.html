About

Kamal Haasan is an Indian actor, filmmaker, playback singer, television presenter, and politician who works mainly in Tamil cinema. He has predominantly appeared in Tamil films and has also worked in Telugu, Malayalam, Hindi, Kannada, and Bengali films.
