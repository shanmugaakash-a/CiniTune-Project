Enthiran

Actor: Rajinikanth

Director: S. Shankar

Description: *Enthiran* (Robot) is a 2010 Indian Tamil-language sci-fi film directed by S. Shankar. Rajinikanth plays Dr. Vaseegaran, a scientist who creates a humanoid robot named Chitti. The film delves into themes of artificial intelligence, human emotion, and morality as Chitti develops the capacity for human feelings, causing dramatic and destructive consequences. *Enthiran* is visually spectacular, with groundbreaking VFX for its time and an engaging story that explores technology's impact on human society.

Rating: 7.1/10
