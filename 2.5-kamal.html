Dasavatharam

Actor: Kamal Haasan

Director: K. S. Ravikumar

Description: Dasavatharam is a 2008 Indian Tamil-language science fiction disaster film in which Kamal Haasan plays ten distinct roles. The story follows a scientist, Govind, as he tries to prevent a deadly biological weapon from falling into the wrong hands, taking the audience through a journey involving different time periods, regions, and characters.
