About

Ilaiyaraaja is a legendary composer down South. He is also an instrumentalist, conductor, singer, and songwriter. Ilaiyaraaja has composed more than 6500 songs and worked in more than 1000 films. He successfully collaborated with singers such as S. P. Balasubrahmanyam, K. S. Chithra, and S. Janaki for some of the greatest hits of his career. He is a recipient of five National Film Awards. He was bestowed with the Padma Bhushan, by the Government of India in 2010, and in 2018, he was presented with the second-highest civilian award - the ‘Padma Vibhushan’.
