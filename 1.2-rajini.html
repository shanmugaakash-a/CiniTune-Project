Kabali

Actor: Rajinikanth

Director: Pa. Ranjith

Description: Kabali is a 2016 Indian Tamil-language gangster drama film starring Rajinikanth as Kabali, an ex-gangster who seeks revenge after being released from prison. The film explores themes of identity, power, and social injustice, as Kabali fights to reclaim his position and reconcile with his lost family. With intense action sequences and Rajinikanth's towering presence, *Kabali* delivers a powerful message of survival and redemption.
